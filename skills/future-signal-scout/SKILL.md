# Future Signal Scout

Purpose: turn a broad topic into grounded future signals that can drive design work.

Use course materials from `https://future-design-lab.github.io/` when the user asks for course sources, cases, or inspiration.

Inputs: workshop topic, place/community/industry, target users, target years, and constraints.

Method:
1. Clarify the topic boundary and target future horizon.
2. Collect 5-8 concrete signals. Avoid generic trend slogans.
3. For each signal, write evidence/source, what changes, who is affected, uncertainty, and design relevance.
4. Recommend 1-2 signals if the user wants guidance.
5. Save the selected signal for `steep-analysis`.

Output:

```text
## Future Signals
| Signal | Evidence / source | What changes | Who is affected | Why it matters | Risk / uncertainty |

## Selected Signal
Signal:
Reason for selection:
Design tension:
Next step: STEEP analysis
```
