# STEEP Analysis

Read first: selected future signal and workshop brief.

Method:
1. Restate the selected signal.
2. Analyze Social, Technological, Economic, Environmental, and Political implications.
3. For each lens, identify what changes, who is affected, and what tension appears.
4. End with 2-3 tensions for local challenge framing.
