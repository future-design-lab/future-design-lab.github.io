# Local Challenge Framing

Read first: workshop brief, selected signal, and STEEP analysis.

Method:
1. Identify the local place, community, institution, or industry.
2. Name the affected people and their current conflict.
3. Convert a STEEP tension into a challenge statement.
4. Make it specific: who, where, why it matters, and what makes it hard.
5. Do not propose a solution yet.

Output:

```text
## Local Challenge
Challenge statement:
Place / context:
Affected people:
Current pain:
Why existing solutions are insufficient:
Connection to selected signal:
Constraints:
```
